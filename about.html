About Our Games

Welcome to Retro Arcade! This website is a simple project created to host classic arcade-style games. Our goal is to provide a fun and nostalgic gaming experience right in your browser.

The Classic Snake game is our first project, featuring levels, local high scores, and a global leaderboard powered by Firebase. We are continuously working on new games and features.

This website and its games were built using HTML, CSS (with Tailwind CSS), and JavaScript.
