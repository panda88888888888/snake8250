Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This document outlines the data we collect and how we use it.

**Information We Collect:**

  • **Anonymous User ID**: When you start a game, we assign a unique, anonymous user ID to track your scores. This ID is not linked to any personal information.
  • **Gameplay Data**: We collect non-personal data such as your scores, the level you chose, and game-end events. This is used to populate the global leaderboard and improve the game.

**How We Use Your Data:**

  • To display a global high score leaderboard.
  • To track general game usage and performance (e.g., how many times the game is played).
  • To provide you with a personalized "last words" message from the snake.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or location. Your data is stored securely in Firebase and is used solely for the purpose of the game.
